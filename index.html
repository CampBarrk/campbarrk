Meet Fallon

Hi, guys! Fallon here!!

Founder & Licensed Veterinary Technician

A little bit about my background — I have been a Veterinary Technician for over a decade. I started as a volunteer in wildlife rehabilitation at The Wild Bird Fund in Manhattan. I fell in love with it — it was hard not to with all of the little goslings.

As I progressed into clinical medicine at Bay Ridge Animal Hospital, I wanted to add to my career, so I became a dog walker at Bark Slope. Eventually I moved over to Marine Park Veterinary Group and worked as their primary surgical technician with a focus in anesthesiology, while continuing my relationship with dog walking as a manager for Brooklyn Bark.

The balance of animal hospitality and veterinary medicine has strengthened my relationship with all species, and I found a love for dogs that I never expected. I feel truly lucky and proud to be where I am now — fortunate to provide the best life possible for both of my pups, my kitty, and all of our furry friends.

🩺 15+ yrs Veterinary Technician 🏔️ Adventure-led care ❤️ Fear-free philosophy
Meet Arthur

Fallon's Dad & right-hand

The gentlest soul at camp

This is my father, Arthur — the kindest, gentlest soul I know. Dad has a big heart for all of our little furry friends, and a special gift for charming even the shyest pups out of their shell.

He's the one who opened my life up to nature, and his unwavering support in every endeavor is what made this whole dream of working alongside animals possible. I grew up in a National Park right here in NYC, so our weekends were spent hiking through the preserve, fishing, and caring for our local wildlife.

His whole family passed down lifelong lessons in valuing our planet. I couldn't be more grateful to call him my Dad.

🌲 Lifelong nature lover 🐾 Whisperer of shy pups
